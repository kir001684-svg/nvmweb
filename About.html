About Us

Nehru Vikas Manav Samiti is a non-profit organization working in the field of social upliftment, empowerment, skill development, and education since 1981. The organization is committed to ensuring equitable access to quality education for children from underprivileged and marginalized communities, particularly in rural and semi-urban areas of Rajasthan. Since its inception, Nehru Vikas Manav Samiti has positively impacted the lives of approximately 15,000 direct beneficiaries.

The Samiti runs Nehru Vidya Mandir Senior Secondary School, affiliated with the Rajasthan State Board, located in Kaman, Deeg District, Rajasthan. The school currently provides education to around 200 needy and economically disadvantaged children, many of whom are first-generation learners. Beyond academic instruction, the school emphasizes holistic development, moral values, life skills, and character building. Over the years, the organization has consistently worked to reduce school dropout rates, with a special focus on girl child education, which continues to be a significant challenge in the region due to socio-economic and cultural barriers.

Our Vision

To build a society where every child can learn, every woman feels strong and independent, and every person has access to basic health and care. We dream of a future where no one is left behind, and everyone can live with dignity, hope, and equal opportunities.
