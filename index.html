Who We Are

NEHRU VIKAS MANAV SAMITI is a non-governmental organization dedicated to making life better for those in need. We focus on helping poor children get an education, supporting women to become strong and independent, and spreading awareness about health and hygiene. Through simple efforts like teaching and organizing health camps, we aim to bring hope, care, and better opportunities to our community.

Our Focus Areas

Creating real change through simple actions

Education

Helping children from underprivileged backgrounds get access to basic education, guidance, and a brighter future..

Healthcare

Organizing health camps and spreading awareness about hygiene to help communities live healthier lives.

Women Empowerment

Supporting women to become confident, independent, and capable through awareness and skill development.
